About CosmoShield AI

CosmoShield AI is an AI-powered Space Weather Prediction Platform developed for the BAH Hackathon 2026. Our system predicts energetic particle radiation before it impacts critical satellite systems using LSTM Neural Networks and NASA space weather datasets.

The goal is to improve mission planning, reduce satellite failures, and provide intelligent decision support for future space missions.
